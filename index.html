About the Studio

Every celebration has a rhythm.
I'm here to capture it.

I'm Harsh — a photographer based out of Indore, working with couples and families across India. My approach stays close to the moment: real laughter, stolen glances, the chaos of the baraat and the quiet just before the pheras.

Over the last several years I've shot weddings in havelis and beachside resorts alike, pre-wedding stories in the hills, corporate and festive events, and personal portrait sessions — always with the same idea: fewer poses, more truth.
